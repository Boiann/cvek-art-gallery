Self-taught Artist

Desire to Create

Since he was a young man, the drive to make and fix was one of his defining traits and desires.

Born in 1969, life growing up was different and challenging. Constant fixing of his children's toys and efforts around the house started giving voice to desire to create, in any way possible. From engine mechanics, botany, farming and building, it seems nothing was impossible for him. And all of it self-taught, learning by trial and error. It seems he only needed to see something to be able to recreate it or make it better. And painting was, and still is, one of his many passions.

Recognition of Talent

His first art exibit, 26.01.2011.

After 10 years of creating stunning paintings, recognition came and first exibit was set up. It took a long while to talk him into it, as he didn't create for recognition, fame or money. It was and still is, a passion. The exibit was an overwhelming success, not to mention how proud and validated the artist felt.

Desire to Evolve

Forever continuing to learn and make.

Is there anything he can't do? Well, according to his loving wife, baking could well be his only nemesis, yet undefeated. But it's never too late.
